Recent Heroic Acts


The death of Tony Stark in "Avengers: Endgame" was a moment that shocked fans. But it also shocked some of the creators associated with the Marvel Cinematic Universe. In fact, one of them tried to convince "Endgame" directors Joe Russo and Anthony Russo from going through with it. In a recent Vanity Fair video, the Russo brothers talked about how Jon Favreau, who directed "Iron Man" and stars as Happy Hogan in the MCU, was against Stark dying. "Part of the pressure came from Jon Favreau, who called us up after he read the script and said to us, 'Are you guys really going to kill Iron Man?'" Anthony recalled of planning Tony Stark's death scene and feeling pressure to do it justice.
